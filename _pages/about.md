I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Science Technology at the University of Houston, with a joint appointment in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

My research develops machine learning and graph-based methods for scientific discovery. I am particularly interested in integrating spatial omics, single-cell genomics, and multimodal biomedical datasets to understand complex biological systems and disease mechanisms.

## Research Interests

* Graph Machine Learning
* Spatial Artificial Intelligence
* Spatial Omics
* Computational Biology
* Multimodal Machine Learning
* Trustworthy AI for Scientific Discovery

## Current Focus

My group develops computational methods for:

* Spatial transcriptomics analysis
* Single-cell data integration
* Graph neural networks for biomedical discovery
* AI methods for understanding cellular interactions
* Reproducible and trustworthy machine learning
